Why to change now?

Since the corona virus situation came along I had a lot of time to think about life, specially about what really matters in it for me.
It just came to me, really clearly, that I´m not happy at my job right now, and it is being like this for a long time now.
Of course is really good to have stability at work get a pay check at the end of the month, but it seems an empty way of living do something that does not bring me joy while I wait for my salary.

I don´t remeber the last time I woke up with a real will to get to get to work... it´s just something I do beacause it have to be done, like cleaning the dishes. Having time to stay at home woth my wife made me realize that I already have everything I nedd to live a meanigfull and happy life. Some things that were really important to my for the last years are losing they meaning... So, why not to stick whith what means and start changing everything else?
I have nothing to lose and, besides that, new experiences are always welcome to me. After all, destiny is the way.
